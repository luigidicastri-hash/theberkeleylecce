Lesley Ray – Swimming For Your Life – 21/11/2025

By Nicolette S. James

Photo by David Katan

In her very interesting talk Lesley explained that, although she had swum since childhood, the impulse to take part in very challenging swims and competitions around the world sprang from a period of an all time low in her life.

Two very different serious health problems drove her to find a distracting activity that would also put her on a path to better health. She found a group of other swimmers who did challenging swims mainly for charity, and this group gave her support, encouragement and companionship.

Thus Lesley has swum in an unusual competition involving the prisoners of the island of Gorgona, (all responsible for very serious crimes) north of Sardinia, another crossing the Hellespont, where she admits that her headstrong character led her briefly astray, and several others worldwide.

She assured us that, however worried she is before tackling a difficult swim, when it comes to the crunch…. she just puts her head down and swims!
