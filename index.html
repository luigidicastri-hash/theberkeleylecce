Welcome to Circolo Berkeley, a cultural non-profit organisation in Lecce. Our mission is to promote arts, literature, cinema and discussion through events, visits and articles.

If you want further information/want to become a member of the Circolo Berkeley, please, send us an email: claudiamazzei1@gmail.com.

You can also come along to any meeting. The first meeting is free, and you can ask about membership at the meeting.

Places are limited. Please book your place using the link provided in the programme.
